Our Story

Redefining housing for a changing world through innovation and flexibility

Who We Are

EVOLLAR was born in 2020 from a simple but powerful idea: housing should adapt to people, not the other way around. Frustrated by the rigidity of traditional real estate models, our founders set out to create a solution that evolves with life's changes.

What began as an experiment in Barcelona has grown into a movement transforming how people think about home ownership. We combine cutting-edge technology with human-centered design to create living spaces that are as dynamic as the lives they support.

Our team of architects, engineers, and community builders work together to challenge conventions and create housing solutions that are sustainable, accessible, and truly responsive to human needs.
